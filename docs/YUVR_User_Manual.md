# YUVR's Invoice - User Manual

Welcome to **YUVR'S Invoice**, your all-in-one professional invoicing and business management platform. This manual provides a crisp, module-by-module overview of the application, designed to help you quickly understand and utilize every feature.

---

## 1. Authentication & Profile Setup
**Purpose:** Secure access to the platform and establish your business identity.

- **Instant Sign-up & Login:** Create an account with a unique username and email. No email confirmation is required—you can log in immediately after signing up!
- **Business Profile:** Set up your company name, full name, and bio to personalize your account.
- **Brand Identity:** Upload your business logo and choose a brand color and custom font. These details automatically populate across all your invoices and estimates.

## 2. The Dashboard
**Purpose:** A centralized command center for your business financials.

- **Financial Overview:** Instantly view your total revenue, pending invoices, and overdue tracking.
- **Quick Links:** One-click access to create new invoices, view pending estimates, or add new clients.
- **Recent Activity:** A fast, real-time snapshot of your most recently generated invoices and their payment status (Paid, Unpaid, Overdue).

## 3. Client Management
**Purpose:** Maintain an organized, centralized database of your customers.

- **Add Clients:** Easily add new clients with details such as Name, Email, Address, and Phone Number.
- **Tax Configurations:** Assign specific Tax IDs, Tax Types (e.g., GST, VAT, NONE), and regional details per client.
- **Smart Autofill:** When creating an invoice or estimate, simply select a client from the dropdown, and all their billing and tax details are populated automatically.

## 4. Estimates & Quotes
**Purpose:** Send professional proposals to clients for approval before finalizing work.

- **Drafting:** Create detailed estimates featuring line items with quantities, descriptions, and unit prices.
- **Statuses:** Track estimates as Draft, Sent, Approved, Declined, or Converted.
- **One-Click Conversion:** Once a client approves an estimate, convert it directly into a live invoice with a single click, completely eliminating duplicate data entry.

> *[Insert Animated Image / GIF here: Converting an Estimate to an Invoice]*

## 5. Invoicing Engine
**Purpose:** Generate, track, and send custom-branded invoices.

- **Sequential Numbering:** Invoice numbers (e.g., `INV-2026-0001`) automatically increment based on your account's history, preventing duplicates.
- **Dynamic Line Items:** Add or remove items dynamically. The system instantly calculates subtotals, applies the client's specific tax rate, and totals the amount in your chosen Currency (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.).
- **Template Engine:** Apply your saved template style (e.g., Elegant Luxury, Modern, Tech Startup) to ensure invoices match your brand.
- **Export & Delivery:** Preview the exact look of the invoice, download it as a high-quality PDF, or email it directly from the application platform to the client.

## 6. Settings & Customization
**Purpose:** Tailor the platform’s aesthetic to perfectly align with your brand.

- **Invoice Templates:** Choose between beautiful, pre-designed templates tailored to different industries (Professional Business, Dark Mode, Creative Agency, etc.).
- **Global Typography:** Select custom fonts that apply to your generated documents.
- **Color Theming:** Set a primary brand color that highlights key elements on your generated PDFs, ensuring a highly premium feel.
