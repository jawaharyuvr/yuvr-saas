STEP-BY-STEP GUIDE SECTION 06

Quick Start Guide

Follow these linear steps to jumpstart your billing pipeline instantly.

01

Configure Your Brand Layer

Navigate to the Settings panel. Upload your agency logo, select a primary brand color (e.g., Orange, Blue), and choose a typography style. Your generated PDFs will instantly inherit these overarching visual characteristics.

02

Insert Your First Client

Head to the Clients module. Input their business name, email, and specific tax requirements (like local GST/VAT logic). This isolates their data securely for future automated dropdown form fills.

03

Draft an Initial Estimate

Create an estimate proposal to send to your new client for approval. Once accepted, strictly utilize the 'Convert to Invoice' software trigger to instantly translate the proposal into a live billable invoice without any data reconstruction.

04

Dispatch the Final Invoice

Navigate to Invoices. Your client is already populated. Simply adjust the final services rendered (custom line items & quantities), and click 'Save'. Instantly export the document as a digital PDF or stream it to their e-mail directly from the platform.
